What is Cooking?

Cooking is the art, science, and craft of preparing food using heat. It can involve a wide range of techniques, tools, and ingredients, and it's practiced in homes and restaurants around the world.

Basic Cooking Tips

  • Always read the recipe first.
  • Prep your ingredients before you start.
  • Keep your knives sharp.
  • Clean as you go.
